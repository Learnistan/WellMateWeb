WellMate is a private, supportive space where you can talk about how you're feeling and care for your wellbeing. This Privacy Policy explains what information the WellMate mobile application (the "App") collects, how we use it, how we protect it, and the choices you have. We built this policy around a simple idea: collect as little as possible, and protect what we hold.

The App is operated by Learn Afghan Inc. ("we," "us," or "our"). By creating an account or using WellMate, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree, please do not use the App.

Our privacy promises

  • We collect as little information as we can.
  • We never sell your information, and we never use it for advertising.
  • Your conversations are private and are used only to support you.
  • We do not track your location.
  • You are always in control of your information.

1. Information We Collect

Account information

When you create an account, we collect your name, email address, and a password (stored only in encrypted/hashed form — we never see your actual password). If you choose to sign in using a social login such as Google or Apple, we receive basic profile information (such as your name and email) from that provider, as permitted by your settings with them.

Movement and activity information

WellMate includes an optional Mindful Movement feature that measures your steps and physical activity. This feature uses your device's motion and fitness sensors (such as the step counter) to estimate movement and distance. It does not use GPS and does not track your geographic location — WellMate does not know or record where you are. This feature is optional; you can use the rest of the App, including the assistant, without enabling it, and you can turn off motion and fitness access at any time in your device settings.

Assistant conversations

WellMate includes an AI assistant that lets you talk about what you're feeling or experiencing. When you use it, the messages you send are transmitted to our AI provider (OpenAI) so that a response can be generated for you. These conversations are used only to provide the assistant's replies to you. We do not use them for advertising, we do not sell them, and we do not share them with anyone for their own purposes. (See Section 4 for more detail.)

Wellbeing information you choose to share

WellMate is a wellbeing app, so you may choose to share information about how you're feeling. We treat everything you share as sensitive and handle it with care, using it only to provide the App's features to you as described in this policy.

Device and technical information

To keep the App working, secure, and reliable, we (and our service providers such as Firebase) may automatically collect limited technical information, such as your device type and operating system, app version, general app-usage and diagnostic data, and crash reports. This information helps us fix problems and improve the App. It is not used to advertise to you.

Information we do not collect

  • We do not collect your GPS or geographic location.
  • We do not use advertising trackers or sell your data to advertisers.
  • We do not build marketing or advertising profiles about you.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect only to:

  • Create and manage your account;
  • Provide and maintain the App's features, including the assistant and Mindful Movement;
  • Send you push notifications you have chosen to receive;
  • Keep the App secure, reliable, and working properly, and diagnose problems;
  • Understand, in aggregate, how the App is used so we can improve it;
  • Comply with our legal obligations.

We do not use your account, activity, or conversation information for advertising, and we never sell it.

3. Legal Bases for Processing

If you are located in the European Economic Area or the United Kingdom, we process your personal information under the following legal bases:

  • Performance of a contract — to provide the App and its features to you after you create an account;
  • Consent — for optional features such as Mindful Movement (motion/fitness access) and push notifications, which you can withdraw at any time;
  • Legitimate interests — to keep the App secure, prevent misuse, and improve it, in a way that does not override your rights;
  • Legal obligation — where we must process information to comply with the law.

Because wellbeing information can be sensitive, where required we rely on your explicit consent to process it, which you may withdraw at any time by contacting us or deleting your account.

4. How We Handle Your Conversations

We know the conversations you have in WellMate can be deeply personal, and we treat them that way.

  • Your messages to the assistant are sent to OpenAI only to generate a reply for you, and are handled subject to OpenAI's data-usage practices for its API.
  • We do not sell your conversations, use them for advertising, or share them with third parties for their own purposes.
  • We do not use your conversations to build a profile of you.
  • Access to conversation data within our organization is limited to what is necessary to operate and support the App.

5. Third-Party Services

WellMate relies on a small number of trusted service providers to operate:

  • Firebase (Google) — provides backend services such as account authentication, secure data storage, push-notification delivery, and app analytics/crash reporting. Firebase processes this information on our behalf, subject to Google's privacy practices.
  • OpenAI — powers the assistant. Messages you send to the assistant are transmitted to OpenAI's API to generate a response, subject to OpenAI's data-usage practices for its API. This is used solely to provide the assistant feature.
  • Social login providers (Google, Apple) — if you choose social sign-in, that provider processes limited profile information to authenticate you.

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to any third party for their own marketing or business purposes.

6. Push Notifications

With your permission, we may send push notifications such as gentle reminders or updates. Notifications are written to be discreet. You can turn them off at any time in your device settings or within the App.

7. Data Retention and Deletion

We keep your information only for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide the App to you, unless a longer period is required by law.

You may request deletion of your account and personal information at any time by contacting us at info@learnafghan.org, or by using any in-app account-deletion option where available. Once we receive your request, we will delete your personal information in accordance with applicable law, except where we are required to retain certain information.

8. Data Security

We use administrative, technical, and organizational safeguards designed to protect your information. Passwords are stored in hashed form, and information transmitted between the App and our services is encrypted in transit. We limit internal access to personal information to those who need it to operate the App. While we take these steps to protect your information, no method of transmission or storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

9. Age Requirements & Younger Users

WellMate is intended for individuals 14 years of age and older. We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone under 14. If you are a parent or guardian and believe a child under 14 has provided us with personal information, please contact us at info@learnafghan.org and we will take appropriate steps to remove it.

If you are between 14 and 18 (or the age of majority where you live), we encourage you to review this policy with a parent, guardian, or another trusted adult, and to use WellMate with their awareness where you are able to. We handle information belonging to younger users with particular care and only as described in this policy.

10. Your Safety

WellMate is a supportive space, but it is not an emergency service and does not replace professional care. If you are in immediate danger or thinking about harming yourself, please reach out to a trusted person, a local health professional, or your local emergency services right away. WellMate can sit alongside that help — it is not a substitute for it.

11. Your Privacy Rights

Depending on where you live, you may have rights over your personal information under laws such as the EU/UK General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) or the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA), including the right to:

  • Access the information we hold about you;
  • Correct information that is inaccurate;
  • Delete your information (see Section 7);
  • Object to or restrict certain uses of your information;
  • Receive a copy of your information in a portable format;
  • Withdraw permissions you have given (such as motion/fitness access or notifications) at any time.

We will not discriminate against you for exercising any of these rights. To make a request, contact us using the details in Section 14. You also have the right to complain to your local data-protection authority.

12. International Users

WellMate is operated from the United States, and our servers and central database are located there. If you use the App from outside the United States — including from Afghanistan, the European Economic Area, the United Kingdom, or elsewhere — your information will be transferred to and processed in the United States, which may have different data-protection rules than your home country. Where required by law, we take steps to ensure such transfers are handled lawfully.

13. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will revise the "Last updated" date at the top of this page and, where appropriate, notify you within the App. We encourage you to review this policy periodically. Your continued use of the App after an update takes effect means you accept the revised policy.
